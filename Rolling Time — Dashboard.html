Email Alerts & Notifications

    

      
Alert Configuration

      
Alerts are sent to larosmike10@gmail.com when a product falls to or below its low-stock threshold. The dashboard checks every hour automatically, and also checks after every stock adjustment.

      

        

        Checking configuration…
      

      
To enable alerts, add your Gmail app password to dashboard/config.json:

      
{
  "alertEmail": "larosmike10@gmail.com",
  "smtp": {
    "host": "smtp.gmail.com",
    "port": 587,
    "user": "larosmike10@gmail.com",
    "pass": "YOUR_GMAIL_APP_PASSWORD"
  }
}

      

        
        
      

    


    

      
Order Notifications

      
An email is sent to sales@rollingtime.co.za automatically every time a new order comes in — whether via PayFast or added manually in the dashboard. The email includes the product, amount, customer name, and order ID.
