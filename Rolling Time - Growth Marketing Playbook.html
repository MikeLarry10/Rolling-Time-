03 - Lead Generation

The Rule of 100.

Pick your primary channel. Do 100 units of activity on it every single day until it produces results. Do not switch channels when it feels slow. Switching is why most people never get traction. The rule: you must do 100 daily for a minimum of 90 days before you judge the channel.

1
post per day
TikTok is your primary growth channel. One short-form video every single day. This is the Rule of 100 applied to content.
100
minutes per day
Minimum content creation time. Filming, editing, writing captions, engaging with comments and DMs. This is the work.
90
days before you judge
You will not see results in week one or week two. The algorithm rewards consistency over time. Commit to 90 days before changing anything.
Lead Channel Priority for Rolling Time

Content first. Warm outreach second. Paid ads third.

  • Content (TikTok + Instagram Reels) — highest ROI, lowest cost, builds the brand permanently. Start here.
  • Warm Outreach — DM every person who likes, comments, or follows. They raised their hand. Treat it like a sales call. Use the CLOSER script in Section 05.
  • Paid Ads (Meta) — only activate once you have 500+ pixel events and at least one organic post that performed well. Use the winner as the ad creative. Starting blind wastes budget.

06 - Platform Strategy

Platform Strategy.

Each platform has a different job. Run them in parallel but do not post identical content on all three. Repurpose, do not duplicate.

Instagram

Your brand home. This is where people discover you, judge your quality, and decide to buy. Every post must make the product look like it belongs next to something that costs twice as much. Feed is your portfolio. Reels are your reach engine. Stories are your daily relationship builder.

TikTok

Your growth engine and your Rule of 100 channel. The algorithm rewards craft and process content. You do not need a large following to reach tens of thousands of people. One good video of wood being stained can outperform months of Instagram work. Post daily. No exceptions for 90 days.

Facebook

Your community and retargeting layer. Less about organic growth, more about holding your audience and running paid ads once the Meta Pixel has data. The 28-45 age group with disposable income is here. Your TikTok best performers go here as paid ads.

07 - Content

Content Pillars.

Every piece of content must do one of two things: attract new eyeballs or deepen trust with existing followers. It should also point — directly or indirectly — at the dream outcome. If a post does not fit one of these pillars, do not post it.

40%

Craft

The making of the product. Wood grain. Hand finishing. The workshop. Before and after. Your strongest point of difference and your biggest trust builder. This content proves the outcome is real.

30%

Product

Clean, beautiful shots of the trays. Individual pieces. The full collection. Detail shots. Your catalogue. This is what converts a warm follower into a buyer. Make it look more expensive than it is.

20%

Culture

Cape Town. The aesthetic of your customer's life. A clean surface. Morning light. The way the tray sits in a real space. This pillar sells the identity, not the product. Your customer wants to be the person who owns this.

10%

Community

Customer reposts. Comments turned into content. Questions. Polls. Behind the scenes of running a small business. Social proof and connection. This is what creates loyalty past the first purchase.

The Hook Formula — Every Caption, Every Video

Lead with the pain. Agitate it. Then offer the solution.

Weak hook: "Our handcrafted tray is perfect for any rolling ritual."

Strong hook: "You've been rolling on a plastic tray from a headshop. It looks like an afterthought. It is an afterthought. There is another option."

The first sentence of every caption and the first three seconds of every video decide whether someone stops or keeps scrolling. Write the hook last, after you know what you are saying. Then make it the first thing they see.

12 - Paid Advertising

Meta Ads.

Do not start paid ads until your organic content has produced at least one winner. Paid ads amplify what already works. They do not fix content that is not working.

When to start

Wait until you have at least 500 website visitors tracked by the Meta Pixel AND at least one organic piece of content that performed significantly better than the others. Use that content as your ad creative.

Ad hook formula

First frame of the video or first line of the image must be a pain hook. "Still rolling on a plastic tray?" or "This is what R420 gets you in Cape Town." Not "Check out our products." That is not a hook.

First campaign structure

Budget: R300 to R500 per month. One ad only. Test one variable at a time.

Format: your best-performing TikTok or Reel repurposed as a Meta ad.

Targeting: South Africa, ages 22-42, interests — woodcraft, handmade goods, artisan products, Cape Town, small business.

Objective: Traffic to product page OR Conversions (once you have 50+ purchase events on the pixel).

Scale rule

Only increase budget when ROAS (Return on Ad Spend) is above 2.0 — meaning for every R1 you spend on ads you make R2 in sales. Check your dashboard. Scale winners. Kill losers after 7 days of data.
